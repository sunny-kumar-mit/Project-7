About us

The Property Registration Search is designed to facilitate seamless access to property registration details across both urban and rural regions, catering to a diverse user base.

For property buyers and sellers, the platform enables verification of ownership, legal status, and registration records for land or buildings, thereby mitigating fraud risks. Real estate agents and developers benefit from a unified interface that accommodates urban searches (using CIN and registration numbers) as well as rural queries (via Survey Numbers and village names), enhancing efficiency and streamlining workflows.

Legal professionals can leverage the system's precise formatting and smart previews to support due diligence and document preparation. Additionally, rural landowners gain a user-friendly experience, allowing even non-technical users to effortlessly verify property records.

By prompting users to input data in the correct format, our platform minimizes errors and enhances data integrity across government databases. Serving as a vital link between the public and official property registries, we ensure faster, more reliable, and universally accessible property search capabilities.
